What is Neighborly and how does it work?
How can I submit a complaint?To submit a complaint, simply navigate to the 'Submit a Complaint' section, fill out the form with your issue details, select a category, and hit submit. It's that easy! You can also attach any necessary files to support your claim.
Can I remain anonymous when submitting complaints?Yes, while providing contact details is optional for follow-up, you can choose to remain anonymous. However, sharing your contact information can help us resolve your issue more effectively.
What types of complaints can I submit?You can submit various complaints related to maintenance, safety, noise, and more. Our dropdown menu makes it easy to categorize your concern for faster resolution.
Is there a way to track my complaint's status?Absolutely! Once you submit a complaint, you can log in to your account to view its status and any updates from community managers. Transparency is key to ensuring every issue is addressed.
How does Neighborly benefit our community?Neighborly strengthens community ties by enabling residents to communicate effectively about their concerns. This platform not only enhances accountability among local authorities but also fosters a spirit of collaboration and support.
